Celebrating 40 Years of Harmonious Worship

Welcome to the official website of the Adult Choir 40th Anniversary

About Us

The Adult Choir has been serving the church with heavenly music for 40 years. Our mission is to lead worship and inspire the congregation through song.

Our History

Founded in 1984, the Adult Choir has grown from a small group to a thriving ensemble that performs at mass, special events, and community outreach programs.

Mission & Values

Our goal is to glorify God through music and serve the community by enhancing worship experiences.

Anniversary Events

Launching (Sun, 13th Oct - 11:00 AM)

A formal ceremony to kick off the 40th anniversary celebration of the St. Francis Desales Senior Choir, with a gathering of the church community and invited guests to commemorate this special occasion.

Sing Song (Sun, 13th Oct - 7:00 PM)

An evening of praise and worship through song, where the congregation joins the choir in celebrating God’s grace with joyful melodies, reflecting on 40 years of musical ministry.

Health Talk (Mon, 14th Oct - 7:00 PM)

A session focused on health and wellness, providing important insights into physical and mental well-being. Aimed at encouraging healthy lifestyles within the church community, led by health professionals.

Church Doctrine (Tue, 15th Oct - 7:00 PM)

An educational discussion on Catholic Church teachings and principles. This session aims to deepen the congregation’s understanding of their faith and its role in daily life.

Social Issues (Wed, 16th Oct - 7:00 PM)

A thoughtful dialogue on current social issues affecting the community. This session addresses challenges, offers solutions, and encourages active participation in social responsibility and justice.

Adoration (Thu, 17th Oct - 7:00 PM)

A time of reverence and worship before the Blessed Sacrament. This session allows the congregation to reflect, pray, and grow spiritually, fostering a deeper connection with God.

Hour of Grace (Fri, 18th Oct - 7:00 PM)

An hour dedicated to seeking God’s blessings and grace through prayers, hymns, and reflection, asking for continued guidance for the choir and the church community.

Health Walk & General Cleaning (Sat, 19th Oct - 4:30 AM)

A community health walk followed by fitness exercises to promote physical well-being. Afterward, participants will engage in a general cleaning of the church premises, fostering teamwork, fitness, and environmental care.

Thanksgiving Mass

A special Thanksgiving Mass will be held to commemorate 40 years of service. All are welcome to attend and celebrate with us.

Unveiling of Anniversary Cloth (Sun, 20th Oct - 11:00 AM)

A grand finale event where the special 40th anniversary cloth will be revealed. This symbolic moment marks the milestone and honors the choir’s contributions to the church over the decades.

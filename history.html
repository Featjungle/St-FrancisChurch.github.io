History of the St. Francis Desales Senior Choir

Our Journey Through the Years

Over the past 40 years, the St. Francis Desales Senior Choir has been a beacon of musical excellence and spiritual devotion. Join us as we look back at the milestones that have shaped us into the choir we are today.

1984 - The Beginning

In 1984, the leadership of Saint Francis De Sales Catholic Church at Assakae initiated the formation of the Catholic Youth in church activities. The youth participated in sweeping, dusting of furniture, and singing in church.

1986 - The Formation of the Choir

Two years after forming the Youth Organization, leaders such as Mr. James Arthur, Mr. Emmanuel Assafuah, and others transformed the youth group into an official church choir. They received support from the St. Anthony of Padua Church at Kwesimintim.

1987 - First Choirmaster Appointed

In 1987, Mr. Kofi Ankaase became the substantive choirmaster, assisted by Mr. James B. Arthur and others. Mr. Ankaase left in 1989, after which visiting choirmasters from St. Anthony of Padua continued to assist.

1992 - First Anniversary

The choir celebrated its first anniversary in 1992, officially introducing itself to the public and solidifying its role in the church community.

2014 - New Leadership

In 2014, Nana Gyansah was transferred from the Star of the Sea Cathedral and became the choirmaster and organist. He was assisted by Frank Noah and Samuel Nhyiraba Arthur (Sammy). Nana Eyiah joined as choirmaster in 2016.

Past Presidents

Past presidents include Mr. Gyimah, Mr. Korankye, Nana Kumi Tea VI, Mr. Anthony Darquah, and Mr. Joseph Amamu. Mrs. Mary Amipamah is the current president.
